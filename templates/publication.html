Publication

We would be delighted to receive your submissions. The submission process is very simple:

  1. Make sure your work complies with the publication guidelines provided below.
  2. Send us your text or proposal (in Word format) along with the completed authorization form to our email: revista.oblivious@gmail.com
  3. Once we confirm receipt, we will evaluate your proposal for inclusion in the next issue of the magazine.
  4. We will contact you again to inform you when and how your work will be published.
